Striking elegance meets bold attitude! This stunning figure embodies style with her tied blouse and flattering faux-leather trousers, captivating the lens with a fierce gaze.

## Prompt

A confident, stylish female figure in a medium-long shot, matching the reference image’s exact facial features, hair, and style. She stands straight with one hand discreetly in her trouser pocket, looking directly at the camera. She wears a black lightweight V-neck blouse tied at the waist to reveal a small portion of the abdomen, with long loose sleeves or flared cuffs. Slim-fitting black faux-leather trousers. Layered thin gold necklaces with subtle pendants.

Makeup includes dramatic cat-eye eyeliner, defined brows, and neutral or slightly reddish lipstick. Hair is long brown with soft waves and lighter highlights, falling naturally over the shoulders and chest.

Set against a dark minimalist studio backdrop in deep black or very dark brown with a smooth gradient. Low-key dramatic lighting: a soft key light illuminating the face and upper body, enhancing clothing texture and hair shine. A subtle warm halo light (amber or orange-gold) glows behind and to the right of her for depth and separation.

Shot on a full-frame camera with an 85mm or 100mm prime lens, aperture f/2.8–f/4 for shallow depth and soft bokeh, ISO 200–400, shutter 1/160–1/250. Large softbox or octabox as the key light, with a grid or snoot for the halo light. High-resolution, chic, modern, stylish, confident low-key studio portrait with dramatic lighting.
