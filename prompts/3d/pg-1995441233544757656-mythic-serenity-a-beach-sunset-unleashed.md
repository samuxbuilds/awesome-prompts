## Prompt

Create an ultra-cinematic, warm-toned beach scene at sunset. Center a young beautiful asian woman standing confidently at the shoreline, looking straight into the camera with a calm, almost mythic energy. She has wet, wavy hair from the ocean breeze and wears a black asymmetrical bikini top paired with a flowing black skirt featuring a high slit. Add an orange, semi-sheer, patterned cover-up that drapes lightly over her arms. Use soft gold jewelry, small earrings and layered pendants, including one large spiral pendant that sits at her chest.

Behind her, show a group of horses running along the edge of the water, their hooves splashing through the waves. Include a mix of dark brown and white horses to give the moment that wild, untamed energy. Let the waves roll in softly with foamy textures, catching the golden sunlight.

Use gentle film-grain, soft contrast, and warm highlights to make the whole scene feel dreamy, earthy, and slightly vintage. Keep the focus sharp on the woman, with the horses and ocean slightly softer to create depth. Aim for a blend of serene and powerful, like a fantasy moment rooted in real-world nature.
