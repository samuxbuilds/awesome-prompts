## Prompt

​A high-resolution, hyperrealistic cinematic portrait of a beautiful East Asian woman in her mid-twenties, seated at a wooden table in a cafe. She has long, voluminous wavy brown hair and is wearing a simple black bandeau top, a gold watch, and a delicate gold necklace. She is resting her chin on her hand and looking directly into the camera with a thoughtful, soft gaze.
​The table setting includes a slice of rich chocolate fudge cake on a white plate, a small glass bowl of golden french fries, and a white teacup with a white rose on the side.
​Lighting: Dramatic, strong golden hour sunlight streams in from the side, casting warm, sharp shadows across her face (chiaroscuro effect). The background is blurred (shallow depth of field).
